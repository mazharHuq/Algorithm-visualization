About ProjectDaVisualizer

Data structures and algorithms are generally considered two of the hardest topics to learn in computer science. But at the same time, these two are fundamental courses in computer science also. So every student should have clear idea and knowledge about data structure and algorithm. Our attempt is to develop a user-friendly visualization tool that visually depicts the processes of algorithms and actions. In this paper, we present a web-based algorithm visualization tool, ProjectDaVisualizer (PDA), that does not require the installation of any additional software
